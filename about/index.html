My story

I'm a Stockholm-based developer with a background in computer science (BSc, Stockholm University) and a deep love for the craft of building things on the web. I've spent several years as a core frontend developer and the main designer of Flowback — an open-source digital democracy platform — while also managing the organisation's finances, leading interns, and contributing to a successful EU Horizon/NGI Trustchain project.

My work spans TypeScript, SvelteKit, React, Python Django, AI/ML integrations, accessible design (WCAG), formal software verification, and UI/UX research. I have a genuine, lifelong fascination with algorithms, mathematics, and the intersection of technology and society. Outside of code I paint, sculpt, and make accessories.

I'm drawn to roles where design and engineering meet — where the question isn't just how to build something, but why it should be built that way. I believe great software is invisible: it gets out of the way and lets people do what they came to do.

What I bring

How I work

Design-led thinking

I approach every interface as a communication problem first. Good design isn't decoration — it's clarity, hierarchy, and trust built pixel by pixel.

Full-stack fluency

From Figma mockup to Django backend, I can own the full vertical. This lets me make faster decisions and communicate clearly with both designers and engineers.

AI-native development

I integrate LLMs, RAG pipelines, and vector databases into real products — not as experiments, but as production-grade features that make tools genuinely smarter.

User research grounding

My BSc thesis and EU project work involved real UX research — surveys, usability scoring (SUS/UEQ), user stories, personas. I don't guess what users need.

Rigorous quality mindset

Formal verification, Pytest, mutation testing, SNYK security scans — quality is baked in from the start, not bolted on at the end.

Ownership beyond code

I've run accounting, written grant applications, managed interns, and handled HR. I understand that software lives inside an organisation, not apart from it.
